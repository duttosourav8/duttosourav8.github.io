Research

Research Interests

My research interests focus on Machine Learning, Explainable AI, Trustworthy AI, and Quantum Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in understanding how intelligent systems make decisions and developing methods that improve their transparency, reliability, and reasoning capabilities.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Designing models and analysis methods that make machine learning decisions more interpretable and transparent.

Trustworthy AI

Improving reliability, robustness, accountability, and reasoning quality in intelligent systems.

Quantum Machine Learning

Exploring advanced learning paradigms at the intersection of AI and quantum computing.

Natural Language Processing

Applying transformer-based models and explainability techniques to language-driven decision systems.

Computer Vision

Developing deep learning methods for medical imaging and high-precision visual classification tasks.

Smart Grid Intelligence

Using machine learning and reinforcement learning for stability prediction, control, and adaptive decision-making.

Long-Term Research Direction

My long-term research goal is to contribute to next-generation AI systems that are both highly capable and trustworthy. I am especially interested in research questions involving interpretability, robustness, human-aligned decision support, reasoning reliability, and the integration of machine learning with emerging computational paradigms.
